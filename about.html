About Hand Pump Nepal

Hand Pump Nepal is a grassroots initiative committed to improving access to clean water and supporting underserved communities in Nepal. Our mission is to install hand pumps in remote areas where water scarcity affects daily life, health, and dignity.

Beyond clean water, we also aim to support widows, orphans, and poor families by providing basic necessities and monthly rations. Every pump installed, every donation received, and every act of kindness makes a lasting difference.

Together, we can build a future where no one in Nepal suffers from lack of clean water or daily essentials.
